Listening to Ballona
explores the intersection of science and art through the use of long-term bioacoustic recordings from the Ballona Wetlands, which visitors can listen to by generating power through pedaling a bicycle. The project underscores the irony of human progress, achieved at the expense of environmental degradation, and reflects on the potential erosion of knowledge due to unreliable future conditions.
